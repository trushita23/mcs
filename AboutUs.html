About Us

The Story Of Our Company

Maurya Converting Solutions is a consulting and service provider organization that focuses on global Converting Industry. We are specialized in selection of required specifications, features and services for machines like Rotogravure Printing, various types of Laminating, Multilayer co-extrusion, Slitting and Pouching etc. in the field of flexible packaging industry.

Our company consists of a team of technical expertise that focuses on providing the best solutions and services to our clients. Our team has an immense experience in Flexible Packaging industry and a substantial amount of experience in below areas :

Area Of Expertise
Marketing-Machinery and Accessories
Upgradation and Customization of machines
Erection & Commissioning
Solventless, solvent base and extrusion lamination
Multilayer and Mono layer co-extrusion
Slitting,Inspection and Doctoring
Supply of spare parts & Vendor Development

We Are

Our team is a conflate of knowledge and skills to undertake converting Green Project,upgradation,customization, modification, troubleshooting, reconditioning, commissioning, shifting and AMC of flexible packaging Indian as well Imported machines. The team is fully trained to identify problems in all the processes and provide quick resolutions while ensuring the quality of the product is maintained.

Additionally, our team has an expertise in vendor development for most of the routine requirement in engineering perspective which is relevant in the converting, paper and plastic industries.
